Branches

Locations around the world where we operate in

Hong Kong

Support!HK is the flagship branch of Support!Int'l. It was founded in 2014 and has since grown to serve over 150 people. The branch has motivated numerous students to make a difference and to work for their community. Currently, Support!HK has 6 dynamic and diverse programme running at 8 schools.

South Korea

Support!SK, founded in 2015, is the 3rd Branch of Support!Int’l to be founded. The branch is led by volunteers from two schools in South Korea. Currently, one programme focusing on english homework help is offered. Plans for new programmes have been outlined and will be executed in the coming year.

Washington DC

Support!DC is the main branch of Support!Int'l in North America. It was founded in 2015 and works with around 50 beneficiaries and 20 volunteers. The team is made up of responsible students from two schools in the Washington DC area. Support!DC offers 2 programmes at 2 different schools.

Hangzhou

Support!HZ is the 4th branch of Support!int’l. It was founded in 2015, and has since, partnered with one school and a museum. They also offer one programme dedicated to helping young chinese students accomplish their goals in english learning.
