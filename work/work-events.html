Youth Leadership Conference

The Youth Leadership Conference is a conference that capitalises on giving back to the community. As one of the few conferences that target highschool students, Support! believes in taking the first steps in making a difference. We aim to redefine what it means to be a youth leader, what it means to give back to the community, and what it means to have a sustainable lifestyle. This conference provides the resources and guidance from various professionals in the field along with networking opportunities with fellow innovators in our local society.
