Dr. Ming Wai Lau, BBS JP

Dr. Ming Wai Lau is currently the Chairman, Commission on Youth. In addition to this role, he is also Chairman of China Estates Holdings, Deputy Chairman of The Ocean Park Corporation and Vice Chairman of Bauhinia Foundation Research Centre. Dr. Lau is a member of the Sichuan Committee of Chinese People’s Consultative Committee in Mainland China, co-opted member of the Community Care Task Force and Social Engagement Task Force, council member of the City University of Hong Kong, and member of the Financial Infrastructure Sub-Committee of the Exchange Fund Advisory Committee. Dr. Lau holds an LLB from King’s College, an LLM from the London School of Economics & Political Science, and a Ph.D in law from King’s College. He is a Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) and a registered attorney and counsellor-at-law in the State of New York.

Ms. Jacqueline Lam

Ms. Jacqueline Lam is currently the Head of Supply Chain Strategy at The Hong Kong Jockey Club, Hong Kong’s largest charitable benefactor. Prior to joining The Hong Kong Jockey Club, Ms. Lam served in numerous management positions at The Airport Authority of Hong Kong, a company she spent 19 years of her career at. In fact, one of Ms. Lam’s proudest honours was to have served in every division within The Airport Authority of Hong Kong, from finance to airport management and operations. Ms. Lam, before The Airport Authority of Hong Kong, was a key part of the Provisional Airport Authority, where she saw the construction of Chep Lap Kok Airport. Ms. Lam is a career chartered surveyor and has been active in the Hong Kong Institute of Surveyors and Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors.

Dr. Theodore S. Faunce

Dr Theodore S. Faunce is currently the Headmaster of Chinese International School (‘CIS’) in Hong Kong, a pre-eminent international school in the Asia Pacific region. He has been serving as Headmaster since August 2006. Prior to joining CIS, Dr. Faunce served as Director of the American Section at Lycée Francais in St. Germain-en-Laye. He is also a director of Teach for China, a founding board member of the Association of Chinese and Mongolian International Baccalaureate Schools and an advisory committee member of the Hong Kong X-Tech Startup Platform. Dr. Faunce holds a B.A. in French and a Ph.D. in Romance Languages from Princeton University. A lifelong educator, academic and linguist, he speaks French and English and conversational Mandarin and Vietnamese.

Mr. Dowson Tong

Mr. Dowson Tong is the President of Social Network Group at Tencent. Mr. Tong started as a technical architect, and led the product development of social network platform of the Company, Qzone. Since May 2012, Mr. Tong has been responsible for the QQ instant messaging and social networking platforms, QQ Music, VIP subscriptions, the open-platform and cloud services. Prior to joining Tencent, Mr. Tong worked in Sendmail, Inc. managing the product development of operator-scale messaging systems. Mr. Tong also worked in Oracle on the development and testing of Oracle Server and Oracle Applications. Mr. Tong received a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Engineering from University of Michigan, Ann Arbor in 1994 and a Master of Science degree in Electrical Engineering from Stanford University in 1997.

Ms. Erica Ma

Ms. Erica Ma is a Co-Founder and Community Advisor of HKCoCoon, a leading technology incubator and co-working space in Hong Kong. Ms. Ma started her career in Silicon Valley, the heart of the startup world. She worked at both the online division of Macy’s and eBay. In 2010 and 2011, Ms. Ma founded Buttermilk Designs Limited. and co-founded the Three Musketeers Group. In April of 2012, along with two other partners, she founded HKCoCoon. In addition to serving as a Community Advisor, Ms. Ma is the Managing Partner at CoCoon Ignite Ventures, the investment arm of HKCoCoon. Ms. Ma continues to advise new startups and work closely with the community development team in creating, managing and running programmes such as HkCoCoon’s signature Pitch Night, Market Meet-up among many others.

Dr. Allan Zeman, GBM GBS JP

Dr. Allan Zeman, widely known as ‘Father of Lan Kwai Fong’, is an active community member. He was formerly the Chairman of the Ocean Park Corporation, currently a member of the boards of the Airport Authority of Hong Kong, West Kowloon Cultural District Authority, Hong Kong General Chamber of Commerce, Canadian General Chamber of Commerce, Cultural & Heritage Commission, Urban Renewal Authority and Tourism Strategy Group for the Hong Kong Tourism Comission. In addition to these roles, Dr. Zeman is a director of Pacific Century Group Limited, Vice Chairman of The Wynn Macau and board member of the Hong Kong Arts Festival and Hong Kong Community Chest. For his ongoing work for the betterment of Hong Kong, Dr. Zeman was awarded the Grand Bauhinia Medal and Golden Bauhinia Star. He is also a Justice of the Peace.

Ms. Rachel Chan

Ms. Rachel Chan is currently the Chief Executive Officer of InnoFoco, a premium marketing, branding and business development consultancy, which she founded in 2007. Since January of 2016, Ms. Chan has been an Adjunct Professor of Values-Driven Innovation at the University of Hong Kong. She has also been the Chief Executive Officer of Junior Achievement Hong Kong, Director of International Marketing & Service Promotion at the Hong Kong Trade Development Council and Senior Consultant to the Hong Kong Design Centre. Prior to joining the Hong Kong Trade Development Council, Ms. Chan spent eleven years in the government administrative service. A highly experienced marketing professional, Ms. Chan has been active in serving the community all throughout her career.
