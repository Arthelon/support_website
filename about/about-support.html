Who are we?

A briefer on the organisation

Support!Int'l is one of the world's first youth-led organisation concerning the issue of education inequities. The entire organisation is led by driven, passionate and caring teenagers who strive for making a difference in their immediate and international communities. We strongly believe that education is the stepping stone to success and our end goal is to try and help those in need reach their full potential and reach their definition of success.
