Who are we?

Founded in 2014, Support! International is a global youth-led education NGO dedicated to bringing innovative educational programmes to different communities in need. Since our founding, we have grown from 1 site and 1 programme to 6 sites operating 9 programmes, each initiated, led and maintained by passionate, driven and caring young leaders. Our mission has continually been to create opportunities and foster connections for individuals from all backgrounds within communities, under the guiding principle and firm belief that young people are tomorrowâ€™s leaders.

Our Mission

Support! was founded under three pillars that we think represent what we stand for and what we hope to achieve in all our endeavours.

Mutual Learning

Every person in Support! is a learner. We believe that we have a lot to offer to our beneficiaries, but also that we have a lot to learn ourselves, and we hope that through our programs, we can create a mutual learning environment and help each other become better.

Youth Leadership

Support! has young people in every role up the ladder, from instructor to leadership roles. We believe that it is our responsibility to give back to the community, and that it is important for young people to use these opportunities to grow as individuals as well as members of society.

Helping Low-income communities

Support! aims to help out the people of Hong Kong who need it most. Very often, such children are disregarded and are not given as many opportunities to flourish as they deserve. Support! wants to change that.
