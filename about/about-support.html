A briefer on the organisation

Founded in 2014, Support! International is a global youth-led education NGO dedicated to bringing innovative educational programmes to different communities in need. Since our founding, we have grown from 1 site and 1 programme to 6 sites operating 9 programmes, each initiated, led and maintained by passionate, driven and caring young leaders.
