International site_name: Singapore International School permalink: /site/sis image_url: sis.JPG --- The Singapore International School programs are run for DHL maintenance and front-line operations staff and their children There are three programs being run here: 1 for the adults, and 2 for the children. The adult students participate in a course where they get the opportunity to strengthen their oral english skills by using them in real-life situations. Whilst, the two programs for the kids focus on building their holistic english skills, as well as sports education. The children participate in hands-on and fun activities, to make their learning cool and enjoyable!
